About Our System

Our Smart E-Waste Management System addresses the critical challenge of electronic waste disposal in residential complexes, academic campuses, and corporate environments. With advanced QR code tracking, automated categorization, and comprehensive compliance reporting, we ensure responsible e-waste management while promoting environmental sustainability.

The system integrates with CPCB and E-Waste (Management) Rules, providing complete traceability and automated reporting for environmental compliance. Our user engagement features encourage participation through sustainability campaigns and green scoreboards.
